About Our History

Sparsh Health Care is dedicated to providing the highest quality of care to patients of all ages, who are dealing with physical limitations or chronic conditions. Our team of experienced physiotherapists is committed to improving the health and wellbeing of our patients through personalized, evidence-based treatments.

At our hospital, you can expect a welcoming and comfortable environment, where your needs and goals are always put first. We believe that every patient is unique and therefore, we tailor our treatments to meet your specific needs.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, combined with cutting-edge technology, help us provide the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Our goal is to help you regain your independence, improve your mobility and reduce pain, so you can get back to doing what you love. We understand that rehabilitation can be a long and challenging process, which is why we work closely with each patient to develop a customized treatment plan that will help you achieve your goals.

  • Quality care
  • Experienced team
  • Comfortable environment
  • Cutting-edge technology
